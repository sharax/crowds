Task: Spam Mail Detection 

Detailed Description: 
You will be given a series of comprehensive text questions. For each question you will be given parts of a mail (Subject+ Crux of body) and asked to classify it as either ‘spam’ or ‘not spam’.

Input type: 
Text (list of mails in the following format - “Subject + Email address of sender”, i.e. not the entire contents of the mail) 

Corpus: Mails taken from http://plg.uwaterloo.ca/~gvcormac/spam/

Representative Tasks Methodology: 

A sample of 20 mails from http://plg.uwaterloo.ca/~gvcormac/spam/ by random sampling methodology
